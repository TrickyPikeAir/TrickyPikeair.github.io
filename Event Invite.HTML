Theme and Decor

1. Theme: Decide on a theme like "Winter Wonderland," "Ugly Sweater Party," or "Classic Christmas."

2. Decorations: Include festive lights, wreaths, Christmas trees, ornaments, and table centerpieces to match your theme.

Guest List and Invitations

1. Guest List: Finalize the number of attendees.

2. Invitations: Send out themed invitations (physical or digital) with details about the date, time, location, dress code, and RSVP info.

Food and Drinks

1. Menu: Plan a menu with festive dishes like turkey, ham, mashed potatoes, and Christmas desserts.

2. Drinks: Include options like eggnog, mulled wine, hot chocolate, and cocktails.

3. Catering or DIY: Decide whether to hire catering or make it a potluck-style event.

1. Music: Create a playlist with Christmas classics
